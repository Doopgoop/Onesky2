Our Story

Born Under the Stars

One Sky is an indie rock band that blends heartfelt lyrics with rich, melodic instrumentals. Inspired by themes of the universe, freedom, and human connection.

Their sound fuses shimmering guitar riffs, rhythmic basslines, and driving drums with vocals that carry raw emotion and energy.

Currently based in , One Sky is building a reputation for powerful live performances and authentic songwriting.

Behind the Music

Arun Tamang (Lead Vocals & Guitar)

Arun’s voice is the heartbeat of One Sky. With a range that effortlessly moves from soulful whispers to powerful crescendos, he brings every lyric to life, connecting deeply with the listener. Beyond his vocal talent, Arun is the creative force that shapes the band’s energy on stage, commanding attention with his charisma and passion. His dedication to his craft and his ability to convey emotion make him not just a singer, but a storyteller, guiding the audience through the atmospheric world of One Sky.

Jasmine Subba (Vocals, Bass & Production)

Jasmine lays down the grooves that give One Sky its pulse. Her bass lines are both steady and inventive, anchoring the band’s sound while adding subtle, dynamic flair. On the synth, she weaves atmospheric textures that transport listeners into the band’s sonic universe, creating layers of sound that feel both expansive and intimate. With her keen musical intuition and experimental spirit, Jasmine shapes the mood of every track, making the music of One Sky feel alive and unforgettable.

Shubham Pradhan (Lead Guitar & Production)

Subham is the sonic architect of One Sky, turning riffs into stories and solos into emotional journeys. With a mastery over tone and technique, he adds both fire and finesse to the band’s sound, bridging melody and rhythm seamlessly. His guitar work elevates every track, creating moments that linger long after the music fades. On stage, Subham’s energy and creativity shine, making every performance a vibrant, unforgettable experience.

Suresh BK (Drums)

Suresh is the heartbeat of One Sky, driving every song with precision, power, and passion. His drumming balances rhythm and creativity, giving each track a dynamic foundation while adding unexpected flourishes that keep listeners hooked. Whether it’s a subtle beat or a thunderous crescendo, Suresh’s timing and energy bring the band’s music to life, making every performance pulse with intensity and emotion.
